Adopt A Leopard Today

Become a World Welfare Zoo adopter and help adopt your own leopard. From just £3 you can help make the difference to these leopards. You can get news and alerts on how well your leopard is doing, with plenty of goodies sent to you along the way.

We have 3 different packages starting at £3, then £20 and £50 per month* adn you can cancel at any time. Each month you will receieve a gift and the monthly WWZ magazine sent to your door, which informs you about the most endangered animals and how we can help.

WWZ adoptions also make a great gift for friends and family members, and after each year you will receive your commemorative WWZ savers badge, so sign up right away!

*Any adoptions requires signing up to direct debit, anyone under 18 require parents permission, cards used require bill payers permission
